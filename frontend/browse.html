Browse



Welcome to the Browse section!

Here you can view word lists directly in Hiragana, Katakana and Kanji, and easily find the words that interest you. All you need to do is press the "Browse" button and then choose whether you want a list of words in Hiragana, Katakana or Kanji.

Once you have chosen this, you will have access to all of the words in that list. Note that in the Kanji list there are different levels. N5 is the beginner level and N1 is the most difficult level.
