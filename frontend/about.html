Learn Japanese is a web application created in 2025 by Sarah, Virgile, Sophie and Orsowen as part of the "Software Project Development" course taught by Professor Davide Picca at the Department of Computer Science for the Humanities at the University of Lausanne, Switzerland.

Students use a number of different applications in their language learning journey. However, in their quest to improve their Japanese language skills, they are often faced with unpleasant features and interfaces which can demotivate them and slow down their learning. In response to this problem, and following a market survey with the stakeholders, who are the students learning Japanese at the University of Geneva in Switzerland, we would like to create a web-app to offer them a more userfriendly, fun, ergonomic and customisable way of making their learning of Japanese more enjoyable.

We hope this web-app can be useful to those learning Japanese. Thank you very much for your time! ありがとうございました!
